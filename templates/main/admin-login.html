About Anurag University

Our initiative introduces India's first online voting system using blockchain technology, ensuring secure and transparent elections. Voters register and verify their identity using Aadhaar cards and OTP verification. Each vote is encrypted and stored as a hash block in the blockchain, preventing tampering and ensuring voting integrity. The system offers election authorities effective administration tools and features a user-friendly interface for easy candidate exploration and secure voting. Real-time accessible election results foster transparency, building confidence among voters and officials..
