MCA

With the use of blockchain technology, this initiative offers India's first online voting system, guaranteeing safe and transparent elections. By uniquely associating each vote with a verified citizen, the procedure is made simpler by using Aadhar cards for user registration and identification through OTP verification. Votes are stored as encrypted hash blocks in the blockchain system, which guards against tampering and ensures voting integrity. Election authorities gain from effective administration tools, while easy candidate exploration and secure voting are made possible by the user-friendly interface. Election results that are accessible in real time promote openness and confidence in both voters and officials.
